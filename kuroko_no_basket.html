Kuroko no Basket

Also known as 'Kuroko's Basketball' and 'The Basketball Which Kuroko Plays'

Kuroko's Basketball, is a manga that was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump. Three seasons of the anime adaption have aired with a movie released in March of 2017. The story centers around the Seirin high school basketball team, with Tetsuya Kuroko and Taiga Kagami as its core players, they face the Generation of Miracles as their opponents in the Interhigh and the Winter Cup.
